Hawaiian Pizza

Description

A delicious classic half your friends somehow don't like.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough - storebought or homemade
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Pineapple
  • Herbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F
  2. Cut and prepare toppings
  3. Prepare dough on pan
  4. Add the pizza sauce, toppings, cheese, and herbs
  5. Bake the pizza until golden brown
  6. Eat and enjoy
