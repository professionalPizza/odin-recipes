Banana Smoothie

Description

A delicious banana smoothie that is perfect for breakfast or a snack.

Ingredients (for one smoothie)

  • Bananas
  • Orange (can be substituted for other fruits)
  • Yogurt
  • Water or milk

Instructions

  1. Peel the banana and cut it into chunks.
  2. Peel the orange and separate it into segments.
  3. Add the banana, orange, yogurt, and water or milk into a blender.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
