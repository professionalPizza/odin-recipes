Energy Bites

Description

These energy bites are a perfect snack for when you need a quick boost of energy. They are packed with protein, fiber, and healthy fats to keep you satisfied and energized throughout the day.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Flaxseed
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chia Seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and mix until combined.
  2. Cover mixing bowl and chill in refrigerator for 1-2 hours.
  3. Roll into 1 inch balls
  4. Serve and enjoy!
